# Privacy Policy for NightChill

**Last Updated:** December 27, 2024

## 1. Introduction

NightChill ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

NightChill is a wellness app designed to help people rebuild structure, discipline, and belonging through connection with anxiety-friendly gyms, mentors, and community support.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** Email address, display name, profile photo
- **Profile Data:** Journey stage, path preference (Spartan/Wellness), bio
- **Check-in Data:** Location check-ins, mood tracking, timestamps
- **Communication:** Messages with mentors, chat history
- **Support Preferences:** Anxiety level, concession eligibility (optional)

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** App features used, session duration, interaction patterns
- **Location Data:** GPS coordinates (only when you check in, with your permission)

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Firebase Authentication:** Login credentials via Google, Apple, or email
- **Google Maps:** Location services for finding nearby support locations

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- ✅ Provide and maintain the NightChill service
- ✅ Track your wellness journey and progress
- ✅ Connect you with anxiety-friendly gyms and mentors
- ✅ Process QR coffee rewards and vouchers
- ✅ Send streak reminders and milestone celebrations
- ✅ Improve our app and develop new features
- ✅ Ensure safety and prevent abuse

We **DO NOT**:

- ❌ Sell your personal data to third parties
- ❌ Share your mental health information with advertisers
- ❌ Use your data for targeted advertising
- ❌ Share your location history with anyone

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Where We Store Your Data

- **Firebase/Google Cloud:** All data stored on Google Cloud Platform servers
- **Region:** Data stored in compliance with Australian privacy laws
- **Encryption:** All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)

### 4.2 Security Measures

- Firebase Authentication with secure token handling
- Firestore security rules limiting access to your own data
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Admin access requires multi-factor authentication
- QR codes are cryptographically signed and time-limited

### 4.3 Data Retention

- **Active accounts:** Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted accounts:** Data permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Check-in history:** Retained for 2 years for journey tracking
- **Chat messages:** Retained for 1 year, then auto-deleted

## 5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Correct:** Update inaccurate information
- **Delete:** Request deletion of your account and data
- **Export:** Download your data in a portable format
- **Restrict:** Limit how we process your data
- **Object:** Opt out of certain data uses

To exercise these rights, contact: **privacy@nightchill.app**

## 6. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

### 6.1 Service Providers
- Firebase/Google Cloud (hosting, authentication)
- Google Maps (location services)
- Analytics providers (anonymized usage data only)

### 6.2 With Your Consent
- Mentors (only information you choose to share)
- Gyms (check-in data at their location)
- Coffee sponsors (anonymous redemption only)

### 6.3 Legal Requirements
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect rights, safety, or property
- To investigate potential violations

## 7. Children's Privacy

NightChill is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

## 8. Mental Health Data Protection

We take extra care with mental health-related information:

- **Mood data** is stored securely and never shared
- **Anxiety preferences** are used only to match you with appropriate resources
- **Journey stage** is visible only to you and mentors you connect with
- **Crisis information** is handled according to duty of care obligations
